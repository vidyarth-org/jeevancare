Privacy Policy



1. Introduction
Jeevan Care respects the privacy of every individual who visits our website or seeks assistance through our platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and handle your personal information in a responsible and transparent manner.

2. Information We Collect
We may collect personal information including but not limited to name, age, contact details, email address, medical-related information (as voluntarily shared), location details, and documents required for coordination with hospitals, NGOs, or government authorities.

3. Purpose of Information Collection
The information collected is used strictly for providing assistance, coordinating medical or social support, connecting users with hospitals or partners, communication, verification of requests, and improving our services.

4. Guaranteed Help & Data Usage
As part of our commitment to provide guaranteed effort and assistance, certain personal information may be necessary to understand and process your request. Information shared with us will only be used to ensure proper guidance, coordination, and support.

5. Medical Information Disclaimer
Any medical or health-related information shared with Jeevan Care is used solely for coordination and support purposes. We do not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment and do not replace professional healthcare providers.

6. Data Sharing with Third Parties
We may share relevant information with hospitals, government departments, NGOs, donors, or partners strictly on a need-to-know basis for providing assistance. We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data to any third party for commercial purposes.

7. Data Security
Jeevan Care takes reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal data from unauthorized access, misuse, loss, or disclosure. However, no method of data transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. User Consent
By submitting your information on our website or through communication channels, you consent to the collection and use of your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

9. Accuracy of Information
Users are responsible for ensuring that the information provided is accurate, complete, and truthful. Incorrect or misleading information may affect our ability to provide assistance.

10. Data Retention
Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by applicable laws and regulations.

11. Cookies & Website Usage
Our website may use basic cookies or analytics tools to understand user behavior and improve functionality. These tools do not collect personally identifiable information without user consent.

12. Children’s Privacy
Information related to minors is collected only when necessary for providing assistance and is handled with additional care and confidentiality.

13. External Links
Our website may contain links to external websites. Jeevan Care is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of third-party websites.

14. Changes to Privacy Policy
Jeevan Care reserves the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Continued use of the website after changes indicates acceptance of the revised policy.

15. Legal Compliance
This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of India. Jeevan Care complies with applicable data protection and privacy regulations.

16. Contact Information
If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, you may contact us at contact@jeevan.care.
