Consent Notice



1. Purpose of Consent
This Consent Notice explains how and why Jeevan Care collects, uses, and processes personal information provided by users while seeking assistance or using our website and services.

2. Voluntary Submission of Information
By submitting your personal, contact, or medical-related information through our website, email, phone, or any other communication channel, you confirm that the information is provided voluntarily and with full understanding.

3. Use of Information
You consent that the information shared may be used by Jeevan Care for assistance, coordination, verification, communication, hospital connections, NGO or government scheme linkage, and other support-related purposes.

4. Sharing with Third Parties
You provide consent for Jeevan Care to share relevant information on a need-to-know basis with hospitals, government authorities, NGOs, donors, or partners solely for the purpose of providing support or guidance.

5. Medical Information Consent
If you choose to share medical or health-related information, you acknowledge that such information is provided with your consent and will be used only for coordination and assistance. Jeevan Care does not provide medical advice or treatment.

6. Guaranteed Help Clarification
You understand that Jeevan Care’s commitment to guaranteed help refers to providing guidance, coordination, and best possible support efforts, and does not guarantee specific medical outcomes, financial aid, or treatment approval.

7. Accuracy of Information
You confirm that all information provided by you is accurate, complete, and truthful to the best of your knowledge. Providing false or misleading information may impact the assistance process.

8. Data Security Acknowledgement
You acknowledge that while Jeevan Care takes reasonable steps to protect personal information, no system or data transmission over the internet can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

9. Right to Withdraw Consent
You have the right to withdraw your consent at any time by contacting Jeevan Care. Withdrawal of consent may affect our ability to continue providing assistance.

10. Legal Acknowledgement
By using this website or submitting your information, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to this Consent Notice along with our Terms & Conditions, Privacy Policy, and Disclaimer.

11. Governing Law
This Consent Notice shall be governed and interpreted in accordance with the laws of India.

12. Contact Information
For any questions or requests related to consent or data usage, you may contact us at contact@jeevan.care.
